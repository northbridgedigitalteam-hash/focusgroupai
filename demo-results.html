Sample Focus Group Results

FitPulse — AI fitness app with personalized 15-minute workouts ($12.99/month)

Study Participants
Mike Chen
Software Engineer, 32
Analytical, data-driven, skeptical of marketing claims, needs proof before purchasing
Sarah Miller
Marketing Director, 28
Tech-savvy early adopter, values convenience, enthusiastic about new tools
Lisa Park
High School Teacher, 35
Budget-conscious, needs simplicity, risk-averse, previous bad experiences with fitness apps
Discussion Transcript
Moderator
"We're launching FitPulse — an AI fitness app that creates personalized 15-minute home workouts based on your schedule and goals. It adapts as you progress and costs $12.99/month after a 7-day free trial. What are your initial thoughts? Would you try it? What concerns do you have?"
Mike Chen
"As a Software Engineer, I need to see data before I believe this works. The 15-minute promise sounds good, but I've tried similar apps that overpromised. I'd need proof of actual results and probably want to see integration with my existing fitness tracking. The price is reasonable IF it delivers, but I wouldn't commit without seeing case studies or metrics first."
Sarah Miller
"I love this idea. As someone who's always busy with work, having AI create workouts for me sounds perfect. I'd definitely try the free trial. My only concern is whether the AI actually understands my fitness level or if it's just generic templates. But I'm excited to test it — the personalization aspect is exactly what I've been looking for."
Lisa Park
"I'm interested but careful with subscriptions. $12.99 adds up, and I've signed up for fitness apps before that I never used. I need it to be really simple — like, open the app and press one button simple. If there's any complexity in setup, I'll lose interest. The free trial is smart though. I'd try it, but I'd need to see value within those 7 days to justify the cost on my teacher's salary."
Strategic Analysis
Key Business Insights
  • Mike represents the skeptical segment — add testimonials, metrics, and integration details to your landing page to convert analytical buyers
  • Sarah is your ideal early adopter — target similar profiles (busy professionals, tech workers) for your beta program and referral campaigns
  • Lisa indicates churn risk — prioritize onboarding simplicity and demonstrate value within the first 3 days to retain budget-conscious users
Recommended Actions

1) Add a "Results in 30 days" guarantee for skeptical users. 2) Create a 60-second setup promise for simplicity-seekers. 3) Build an early adopter referral program. 4) Price point is acceptable across all segments — no changes needed.
